❮ Båken- Panorama terrace

8250 Egå, Denmark

-2° Cloudy

Not busy

Depends on you

Nestled in the heart of Aarhus lies a hidden special place for nature lovers - a natural haven with a wooden building designed for birdwatching experiences. Tucked away from the bustling city, this secret retreat offers a perfect blend of serenity and wildlife exploration.

Best time to visit: 15 February- 15 November
