Discover a charming park in Aarhus with three cozy wooden buildings, creating a unique and tranquil space for nature lovers. Tucked away from the city's hustle and bustle, this serene park offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and relaxation.

Best time to visit: 15 March- 15 November
