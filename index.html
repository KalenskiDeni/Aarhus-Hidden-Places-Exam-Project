6 Important Tips before going outside:

1. Plan your adventure

Research the area, check trail maps, and be aware of any regulations before you go.

2. Tell someone about your plans

Inform a friend or family member about your outdoor plans, including your destination and expected return time.

3. Pack Essentials

Bring essentials such as water, snacks, a map or GPS device, a first aid kit, and any necessary permits. Be prepared for unexpected situations.

4. Wear Suitable Clothing

Dress in layers and wear appropriate footwear for the terrain. Consider the season and potential weather changes during your outdoor activity.

5. Respect Wildlife and Plants

Keep a safe distance from wildlife, and avoid disturbing plants. Respect the natural environment and follow the rules.

6. Check the Weather

Before heading out, check the weather forecast for the day and be prepared for any changes in weather conditions.
